MedFlo

About MedFlo

MedFlo is a cloud-based, real-time health response system that integrates ambulance and hospital operations, enabling seamless data sharing, efficient resource management, and improved emergency care outcomes.

Features

Real-Time Data Sharing

Sync patient status, ETA, and care needs between ambulances and hospitals.

Patient Record Access

Enhanced and secured access to patient medical histories for paramedics and hospital staff.

Communication Hub

Streamlined communication for EMS teams, hospital staff, and patient.

Analytics and Insights

Provide data on response times, resource usage, and patient outcomes to identify bottlenecks and optimize workflows.
